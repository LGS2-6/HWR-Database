Hertfordshire Support Directory
"The following is a database displaying the organisations that offer support/aid/advice to vulnerable people, whether that be Refugees, Asylum Seekers or people facing hardship. The label "Refugees" refers to organisations that offer support to refugees, and the same rationale applies to the label "Asylum Seekers". The label "Anyone" means that the support offered by any given organisation is not exclusive to Asylum Seekers or Refugees. Note that some organisations require the referral of Herts Welcomes Refugees and thus an area convenor or a trusted member of HWR must be notified if you access or want to access the support of some of the organisations listed below.

One-time setup: connect your Apps Script URL

This directory loads live from your Google Sheet via a small free Google Apps Script relay (avoids the browser CORS errors you hit before). Setup takes about 3 minutes — full instructions are in the accompanying guide.

  1. Open your Google Sheet → Extensions → Apps Script
  2. Paste in the provided script code, click Deploy → New deployment → Web app
  3. Set access to Anyone, click Deploy, and copy the Web App URL it gives you
  4. Paste that URL below
